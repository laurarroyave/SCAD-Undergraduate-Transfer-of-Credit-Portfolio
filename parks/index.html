National Parks – America's Natural Legacy

The national parks of the United States stand as shinning representations of America's historical and natural heritage. With nearly 400 parks within the system, these national treasures reflect pride in the preservation of the nation's history and a protective stewardship of unique natural areas. Each site is distinct in character yet bound to others within the system as a resource managed and preserved for the benefit of the nation's citizenry.

Whether you are planning a trip to one of America's national parks or are simply interested in learning more about these special destinations, this Web site intends to showcase some of these cherished places and inspire those who visit to take pride in these majestic and inspirational resources that are owned by all of us.
