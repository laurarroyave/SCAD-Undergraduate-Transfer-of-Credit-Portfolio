Brief description about Colombia:

This country is located in South America. Its neighboring countries are: Brazil, Ecuador, Panama, Peru, and Venezuela. It's capital city is Bogotá located in the state of Cundinamarca.

Colombia's main economic activity is Agriculture but it's main crop is coffee. The country's climate is tropical and isothermal. Being located near the Equator, Colombia has variations within five natural regions and depending on the altitude, temperature, humidity, winds and rainfall.


Some of the agricultural products of Colombia are:

  1. Cut Flower
  2. Bananas
  3. Sugarcane
  4. Cocoa Beans
  5. Panela
  6. Tobacco
